The Headboss Standard

Founded in 2021, Headboss Treatment was born from a frustration with the binary choice modern men faced: a quick, impersonal chop shop or a sterile, clinical spa. We saw the need for a third space—one that respects the craft of traditional modern barbering while embracing the necessity of physical restoration.

Our barbers dan Therapist aren't just stylists; they are trained in therapeutic massage techniques. We understand that the tension you carry in your neck and shoulders affects your posture, your presence, and your mindset.

When you step out of our chair, you don't just look sharper. You feel lighter. You stand taller. You are ready to lead.
